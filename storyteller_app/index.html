StoryBridge - Oral Histories

A storytelling platform for sharing oral histories across cultures. Using AI to complete:

  • Speech to text: Transcribes spoken stories.
  • Language Detection: Identifies the language of the storyteller.
  • Translation: Makes stories accessible globally.
  • Named Entity Extraction: Highlights cultural references and historical figures.
  • Sentiment Analysis: Captures emotional tone of narratives.
